LARGEST MEAT PRODUCER

Sadeeq agro was established in 2010 with a view to produce good quality world class meat and Dairy products for the local and international consumers. Within 8 years Sadeeq Agro becomes pioneer setting a largest cattle farm house in the country. Through research and observing market demand Sadeeq Agro is working continuously improving new and quality products. All products we produce in our own farm house and sell to customers as retail and wholesale basis with extensive care. Ensure the safe and best quality food is our prime concern.
